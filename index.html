FORESIGHT

About

20% of car crashes are caused by fatigue. Yet, studies show that people can't always tell when they're tired.

With Foresight, we aim to solve this problem by providing glasses that detect whether or not you're tired.

Navigate this site's various pages or check the card below for instructions!

How to Use

Foresight is easy to use, coming with its own applications for data collection and analysis.

Once you make an account, you can use these to track your tiredness based on how often you blink!
